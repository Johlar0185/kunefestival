19 - 23 July
Ungdomsoen

KUNE Festival is a yearly 5-day art & music festival on an island 30 minutes away from Copenhagen, providing a creative environment with 5 stages, 2 art galleries, outdoor installations, and workshops. We aim to connect artists, audiences and other cultural actors in the underground cultural landscape of the Nordic region.
